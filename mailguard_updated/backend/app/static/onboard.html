How it works

1
Click "Connect Microsoft 365 Tenant"
You're redirected to Microsoft's login page. Sign in with a Global Admin account for the tenant you want to scan.
2
Review and accept permissions
Microsoft shows you exactly what MailGuard is requesting — all read-only. Click Accept.
3
MailGuard sets itself up automatically
In the background, MailGuard creates a secure app registration in your tenant with minimal permissions.
4
Get your security score
Your first scan runs automatically. Results appear in about 90 seconds with a full breakdown and remediation steps.

What we access

Directory.Read.All Read users and admin role assignments
Policy.Read.All Read Conditional Access policies
AuditLog.Read.All Check MFA registration status
Domain.Read.All Read DNS records for SPF/DKIM/DMARC
Exchange.ManageAsApp Read Exchange Online security policies
✓ Read-only — MailGuard cannot modify anything
